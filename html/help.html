Welcome to Budget App

Budget App is a shared household budgeting tool. All accounts, transactions, and categories are shared across all signed-in users in your household.

Importing transactions

  1. Go to Import in the sidebar.
  2. Select the destination account from the dropdown.
  3. Download a CSV from Bank of America and drop it into the upload zone.
  4. Click Import Transactions — duplicates are skipped automatically.

Tips

  • Transactions, accounts, and categories are shared by all users — changes are visible to everyone in your household immediately.
  • Use the search bar on the Transactions page to quickly find a specific purchase.
  • Toggle between light and dark mode using the sun/moon icon in the top-right corner.
  • The ? button in the header always brings you back to this page.
