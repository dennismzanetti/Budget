Getting started

Welcome to Budget App

Budget App is a shared household budgeting tool. All accounts, transactions, and categories are shared across all signed-in users in your household.

Pages overview

Dashboard
A summary of your budget targets, recent transactions, and monthly cash flow.
Budget
Set spending targets for each category and track progress for the current month.
Transactions
Browse, filter, and search all transactions across every account. Filter by account, category, type, date range, or keyword.
Accounts
Add and manage your financial accounts (checking, savings, credit cards, loans, etc.).
Categories
Create and manage spending categories. Assign a color to each for easy identification in reports and charts.
Reports
View monthly summaries, spending trends, and cash flow breakdowns.
Import
Import transactions from a Bank of America CSV export. Select the destination account before uploading.
Settings
View your signed-in profile and recent repository commits.

Importing transactions

  1. Go to Import in the sidebar.
  2. Select the destination account from the dropdown.
  3. Download a CSV from Bank of America and drop it into the upload zone.
  4. Click Import Transactions — duplicates are skipped automatically.

Tips

  • Transactions, accounts, and categories are shared by all users — changes are visible to everyone in your household immediately.
  • Use the search bar on the Transactions page to quickly find a specific purchase.
  • Toggle between light and dark mode using the sun/moon icon in the top-right corner.
  • The ? button in the header always brings you back to this page.
